Volleyball

There are 7 different positions in Volleyball. At one time, 6 people are on the court. Different positions on are different parts of the court and have a special part to play in the game.

The different positions are the outside hitter, right side hitter, opposite hitter, the setter, middle blocker, the libero, and finally a defensive specialist.

The Middle Blocker

  • At the net
  • In the the middle of the court
  • Involved in blocking the oppenents hits
  • Also invloved in hitting the ball

The Setter

  • Plays defense in the back row and if they get the first pass, they need to make sure another person can set for them
  • Once they play defense in the back row, they go in front to the net to be ready to set the ball for a teamate to spike the ball
  • They also block hits from their oppnents from the right side

The Libero

  • Wears a different colored jersey
  • Does all the passing/digging from backrow because they can only stay in back row
  • Can go in and out for any player in back row and they usually go in for the middle blocker when they rotate for the back row
  • Stays in almost the entire game

Right side hitter

  • On the right side of the court
  • Main thing to to hit and block from the front row but they also play back row on the right so they need go defensive skills

Opposite Hitter

  • Hit the ball the most out of all players and also block the ball
  • Are positioned in the front left but also play back row on the left

Outside Hitter

  • Do the passing with the libero in the back left side of court
  • Also plays front row on the left front where the hit and block the ball

Defensive Speacialist

  • Can sub in for any player on the court
  • Can play front and back row because they theorticaly play any position on the court
